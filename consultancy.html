Mitigating Risk in Solar Assets

The renewable energy sector presents distinct challenges in regulatory compliance, land acquisition, and financial modeling. Unforeseen technical or policy shifts can significantly erode project IRR.

Helios Solar provides institutional-grade Strategic Advisory. We execute rigorous technical audits, market intelligence reporting, and debt syndication to transform land banks into high-yield, grid-connected infrastructure.

Advisory Competencies

End-to-end solutions for Greenfield and Brownfield developments.

Capital Structuring

We facilitate Non-Recourse Financing and Equity Syndication. Our team develops bankable financial models (DSCR, LCOE sensitivity analysis) to secure competitive funding from DFIs and national lenders.

Regulatory Compliance

Expert navigation of CERC/SERC regulations, including Open Access policies, CEIG approvals, Land Use Conversion (NA), and connectivity agreements with CTU (ISTS) or STU networks.

PPA Negotiation

Structuring robust Power Purchase Agreements (PPA) for Group Captive or Third-Party Sale models. We ensure terms protect long-term revenue stability against tariff volatility.

Techno-Commercial Audit

Independent validation of engineering designs, PVSyst yield reports, and technology selection (BOM) to minimize technical risk and optimize CAPEX efficiency.

Market Intelligence

Policy Optimization

The renewable policy framework is dynamic. We leverage regulatory foresight to optimize project structures, ensuring compliance and maximizing fiscal incentives.

  • ISTS Waiver Strategy

    Strategic project siting and sizing to qualify for Inter-State Transmission System charge waivers.

  • ALMM Protocols

    Ensuring procurement strictly adheres to the Approved List of Models and Manufacturers (ALMM) to prevent commissioning delays.

  • Group Captive Equity

    Structuring precise 26% equity holding patterns to legally waive Cross-Subsidy Surcharges (CSS).
