What is a Solar IPP?

An Independent Power Producer (IPP) generates electricity for the purpose of selling it to utilities or end-users (C&I clients) rather than for self-use.

At Helios, we handle the entire lifecycle: from land acquisition and financing to construction and maintenance. You simply sign a Power Purchase Agreement (PPA) and pay a fixed tariff typically lower than the grid rate.

  • Zero Capital Investment
  • Guaranteed Savings
  • Risk-Free Operations
